Class Format

Each week, there are 2 lectures and 2 tutorials. The 2 tutorials have the same contents so students only need to attend 1.

Getting Help

If you have a question, to get a response from the instructor quickly we strongly encourage you to post it to Slack or WeChat Group. For private matters, please send a private mesage in Slack only to the course instructor. For longer discussions and to get help in person, we strongly encourage you to come to office hours. You can also reach out to us via email using the course staff mailing list.
